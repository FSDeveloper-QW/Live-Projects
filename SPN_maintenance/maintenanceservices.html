701_07_AUDITING

service category

Business support Services

detailed service description

An audit is an independent examination of all the financial information, transactions and source documents of any business for a specific period of time (usually 12 months / financial year), whether the business is profit oriented or not, and irrespective of its size or legal status. Such an examination is conducted by a registered auditor with a view to express an independent opinion on the company's performance for the financial year under review and result in the Drafting and finalisation of Annual Financial Statements that attempts to ensure that the books of accounts are properly maintained by the company as required by law. This excercise is usually done once a year by a company and could take from 2-8 weeks, depending on the size of the company and the amount of transactions. This is usually a once-off annual engagement between the Client and the Auditor, with the payment usually a fixed once-off fee for the Annual Auditing Services and Drafting of the Annual Financial Statements.
