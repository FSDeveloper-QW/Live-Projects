detailed service description

This outsourced service entails the day-to-day capturing of all financial transactions of your business by a professional accountant, inclusive of invoices, payments and receipts, while also recording any other associated accounting functions on a dedicated accounting software package. The services further include monthly and periodic statutory compliance and submissions, reconciliation of all bank accounts, monthly management accounts and reporting. This is usually a 12 month engagement between the Client and the appointed Professional Accountant, with the payment usually a fixed monthly fee in advance, for the required accounting services to be rendered. This service could also include at an addtional negotiated, the Drafting of Accounting Policies and Procedure Manuals, Standard Operating Procedures (SOP's), as well as HR and Payroll Services, with their periodic associated statutory compliance and submissions as required by law.
