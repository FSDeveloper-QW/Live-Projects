Kettie Meisie (Pty) Ltd was established in 2021 as the first of many coming clothing ranges that will operate under the SHOPMAX Group. All of our planned clothing ranges operate as independent business entities, managed by an appointed CEO and design team, which share offices, staff, operational resources, as well as the the e-commerce and logistical platforms of the SHOPMAX Group.

"Kettie Meisie" is a ladies clothing brand with a niche focus on Afrikaans speaking women, while promoting sound principles and values underlying the essence of being that unique and special women in the lives of many. All communication of this brand is conducted exclusively in the Afrikaans language. More details about the brand, resellers across South Africa, contact details and the current product offerings are presented below.

The "Kettie Meisie" Story

Our Story

"Kettie Meisie" is someone who believes in herself, listens to her inner voice and follows her true north. No two "Kettie Meisie"'s north are the same, and that makes her unique and special. Her inner beauty of being human and caring for others, she lives out humbly and unconditionally on a daily basis. The "Kettie Meisie" clothing range represents that search for her true north, which is inspired by the choices of her heart, the daily decisions she makes and the fearless steps she takes towards her true north ...
