skynamo crm

SKYNAMO is a dynamic sales management solution loved by both sales representatives and sales managers alike. It is a robust application that simplifies managing your sales team and complete sales processes with cutting edge features. Sales reps use Skynamo to enhance their relationships with their customers and to minimize time wasted on admin, while sales managers log into an online dashboard to keep track of what is happening in the field and get reports on the performance of the sales staff that report to them.

SHOPMAX is honoured to be the preferred Tablet and Smartphone Distribution Partner for Skynamo. It remains our goal to perpetually provide friendly advice and device information to their respected clients, to ensure remarkable after sales service in terms of device problems and standard device warranty policies, while we strive to always negotiate exceptional discounts with our national suppliers on all Skynamo compliant devices for both Skynamo and its clients. We also have the logistical muscle to deliver the selected devices to their client's doorstep anywhere in the country at reduced rates and within 3-7 Business Days from receiving full payment for all ordered devices.

benefits for sales managers

gain clarity and control

Skynamo provides a real-time view of sales and sales activity, so you can make smarter sales decisions and coach your sales team to sales success.

know exactly what's happening in real time

Skynamo automatically captures routes driven, customers visited, and time spent with customers, and makes it easy for reps to capture orders, make notes on your retail execution and other vital customer notes. Daily reports are generated automatically and available in real-time.

make smarter sales decisions

Skynamo's reporting features deliver vital sales information you can use to make your sales team more productive and more effective. Who are your top performers? Which customers are your most profitable? How are sales this month vs. last month per customer? Skynamo delivers these answers and more.

coach your team to success

Assign tasks to reps and add notes to their timelines, and ensure the right customers are visited with the right frequency.

forecast monthly sales

Get a real-time view of orders submitted and invoiced compared to your monthly or annual target per sales rep.

benefits for sales reps

reduce admin and sell more

Skynamo's mobile sales app automates mundane tasks, minimizes data entry and cuts out all the admin, so field sales reps are freed up to spend more time selling.

automated reporting

No need to spend hours each week manually compiling daily or weekly reports. Skynamo automatically tracks time on the road, distance travelled, routes driven, time with customers, customers visited, notes taken, and orders submitted.

improved customer engagements

Never walk in unprepared again. Skynamo lets you start customer engagements with full knowledge of all previous interactions and info - even if you're taking over an account for a colleague. All information is available - even offline.

reduced drive time

Live customer location and route planning - See on a map where all your customers are located in relation to you, so you can plan your day better and reduce driving time. Even see which customers are overdue for a visit so you can prioritize them for a visit.

Colorful digital catalogs makes selling and ordering easier

No more lugging around out-dated printed catalogs. Share the latest product info and availability with your customers via a full-color digital catalog on your mobile device.

Capture orders faster and more accurately

No need to call the office to check on pricing or product availability. Get your orders right the first time around. Skynamo gives you access to stock, pricing and discount information per customer.

Shorten order fulfilment time

No need to head back to the office to submit orders. Submit digital orders on the road to reduce turnaround time on orders and ensure order accuracy the first time.

Improve your sales performance

Set your own weekly goals to help you reach your monthly targets. Mobile analytics helps you plan better, make better sales and be more motivated in your day-to-day activities
